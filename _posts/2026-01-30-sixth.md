## Excellent Poster Award at KCS 2026

<br>

At The 33rd Korean Conference on Semiconductors (KCS 2026), I received the **Excellent Poster Award** in the **T. AI Division**.

The awarded poster was titled:

“A Schmitt-Trigger Comparator-Based Noise-Robust Reconfigurable Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Neuron Circuit for Spiking Neural Networks”

The work presents a Schmitt-trigger comparator-based reconfigurable leaky integrate-and-fire neuron circuit for noise-robust spiking neural networks.
